### Overview

**Payment Orchestration** (also known as **Payment Infrastructure-as-a-Service** or **PIaaS**) is our **enterprise payment orchestration Africa** solution designed for fintechs, large enterprises, and businesses that have or want to establish **direct contracts with Mobile Network Operators (MNOs)** and Payment Service Providers (PSPs).

Payfonte provides the **payment orchestration API** technology layer—API, dashboard, reconciliation engine, and **smart payment routing Africa** logic—while you maintain ownership of your provider relationships and negotiate your own rates for **MTN MoMo API integration**, **M-Pesa API**, **Airtel Money API**, and other **mobile money API** connections.

## Payment Aggregation {#payment-aggregation}

### Overview

**Payment Aggregation** is Payfonte's turnkey **mobile money payment aggregator** solution for businesses that want to **start accepting local payment methods in Africa immediately** without establishing direct contracts with MNOs. We act as the aggregator, providing access to our pre-negotiated partnerships across all supported markets for **MTN MoMo API integration**, **M-Pesa API**, **Wave Money integration**, **Airtel Money API**, and other **alternative payment methods (APM)**.
